Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 26,2024

Welcome to Smart Water! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using our application, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

Email address, username, and password: Collected during account registration to authenticate and manage your access to the application.

b. Automatically Collected Data

• Usage data: Information on how you interact with the application (e.g., feature usage and access times).

• Location data: Used to suggest optimal locations for fog and rainwater harvesting via MapBox API.

c. Third-Party Data

• Weather data: Retrieved from OpenWeather API for forecasts and predictions.

• Map data: Accessed through MapBox API for location-based features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

• Provide and maintain the functionality of Smart Water.

• Personalize your experience based on your location and preferences.

• Generate and display predictions for fog likelihood and water harvest potential.

• Improve application performance and user satisfaction.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Your information may be shared only as follows:

• Service Providers: With OpenWeather and MapBox APIs to provide functionality.

• Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect the safety and rights of users and Smart Water.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your information securely and use industry-standard measures to protect it against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

• Access and review the personal data you provide.

• Request the deletion or correction of your information.

• Opt out of data collection for specific purposes by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Policies

Our application integrates OpenWeather and MapBox services, which operate under their own terms and privacy policies. Please review them to understand how your data is managed outside of Smart Water.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated via email or through the application. Continued use of Smart Water after changes are implemented constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

SmartWater

Smart Water is a capstone project developed as part of the Computer Science program, integrating machine learning and environmental sustainability to optimize fog and rainwater harvesting in Bacoor, Cavite.
