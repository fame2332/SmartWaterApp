About SmartWater

SmartWater was developed by three innovative students from De La Salle University-Dasmari√±as, driven by a shared passion for sustainability and community impact. This project aims to optimize the collection of rainwater and fog, offering a practical solution to water scarcity in Bacoor, Cavite. Inspired by the growing challenges posed by rapid urbanization in the area, SmartWater is designed to address the increasing demand for sustainable water sources. The system combines advanced technology with environmental responsibility, ensuring an efficient and eco-friendly approach to water management. Through SmartWater, The founders strive to empower Bacoor residents with a reliable alternative to combat water scarcity and build a more sustainable future.

Purpose

SmartWater aims to address the pressing issue of water scarcity in Bacoor, Cavite, by harnessing the natural resources of rainwater and fog. Through an innovative and sustainable water collection system, it provides an eco-friendly solution to the growing demand for water in rapidly urbanizing areas.

Goal

The goal of SmartWater is to empower the community by offering a reliable and sustainable water source, reducing dependency on traditional water supply systems. By integrating advanced technology with environmental consciousness, the project seeks to promote long-term water conservation and contribute to a more resilient and self-sufficient Bacoor.

Features of the App

Weather Overview:

Displays current weather and forecast (temperature, humidity, fog intensity, rainfall).

Water Collection Dashboard:

Shows real-time data on potential water collection rates.

Detailed View:

Displays weather variables (humidity, temperature, wind speed) with specific water collection estimates.

Topographical Insights:

A map showing the best spots for water collection based on terrain features.
